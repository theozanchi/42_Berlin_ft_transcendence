to our ponger puff girl. There are a lot of things to do in this app. You can either play local in split-screen or create an user accoutn to play remote with your friends and peers!

How to Play

Welcome to the ultimate 3D pong. Be aware that the ball can bounce once of a wall. You can both move your paddle and rotate the cube. Good luck.

Use the following keys to control the game:

  • Arrows: Move the paddle
  • WASD: Rotate the cube
  • Spacebar: Shoot the ball
